Choose Obsidian mode if you use Obsidian for fewer broken image links.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl+Shift+S Save to Folder

Extracts the article, downloads images, and writes everything into your chosen folder.

Ctrl+Shift+C Copy to Clipboard

Copies the full article as Markdown (images keep remote URLs).

Ctrl+Shift+P Save as PDF

Opens the article in a print-ready page and triggers Save as PDF.

You can change these shortcuts at chrome://extensions/shortcuts
